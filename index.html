    Order Issues

    "Why my order was cancelled and refunded?"
    We will send you an email notification about order cancellation, it may because:
    1. Your order was canceled automatically within 12 hours due to payment timeout.
    2. We may cancel your order if we didn't receive the documents using for verifying your payment as mentioned in the previous email.
    3. When items of your order are not available, we may cancel your order if we didn't
    receive your replacement request. You may repurchase or choose something else for checkout.


    "How can I place an order?"
    Thank you for choosing SHEIN! We're happy to show you how to order items on our site.
    Step 1: Please register for a SHEIN account.
    Step 2: Choose the item size, color and click on the [add to bag] button.
    Step 3: 【View BAG】 and check out with your debit/credit card, Paypal, Afterpay, Klarna, etc.


    "Why do orders in my account disappear?"
    If you didn't find your order in your SHEIN account, it may because:
    1. The order may be deleted by accident.---you can restore the deleted order from 'Deleted Order History'.
    2. You may log in to the wrong account, we suggest you can re-try with the other login method, such as 'Facebook' or 'Google'.
    Additionally, you may Contact Agent for help.

    Return & Refund

    "How long does it take for refunds to be processed?"
    Returns will be processed within 7 business days upon receipt of your package, as we need to check the status of the returned items, and what we need to improve.
    Different refund methods have different refund processing times.


    "What is the status of my return or exchange?"
    It will take up to 7 days to process your refund or exchange request after we receive your package.
    For more info about the return status, please refer in your SHEIN account.


    "Can I get full refund for the return?"
    We will offer a full refund for all eligible items, excluding shipping fees, shipping guarantees and non-refundable items: bodysuits, lingerie, jewelry, beauty, underwear,
    event&party supplies, DIY supplies ,pet supplies, accessories (except scarves, bags, and mermaid blankets), items with non-returnable marks and free gifts.
    Also we cannot offer refunds on cosmetic if the hygienic seal has been broken.Coupon codes and points will not be refunded as they do not carry monetary value.

    Payment & Promos

    "Why did I receive a charge notification after I recieved my refund?"
    We apologize for any confusion this may have caused.
    If you noticed a charge on your bank statement after you received a refund, it may be because you requested a refund immediately after the payment successfully went through.
    The bank's transaction period for processing refunds is faster than the transaction period for processing charges,
    which is why you received the charge notification after the refund notification.
    We recommend contacting your bank for exact details regarding your transaction. Thank you for your cooperation.


    "How can I cancel or modify my SHEIN E-gift card order?"
    You can cancel it in [My Orders]-[Gift Card Orders]. The money will return to your gift card balance within 24 hours.
    For more info, please refer to the SHEIN Gift Card Terms and Conditions.
    Note: For orders placed before Aug 1st, please contact agent for better help.


    "Why can't I pay with SHEIN E-gift cards on check out page?"
    There are 3 reasons which may caused SHEIN E-gift cards payment failed:
    1. Wrong password and PIN
    Please double check your SHEIN E-gift cards number and PIN before enter
    2. SHEIN E-gift cards locked
    Be notice that Entering the wrong PIN many times in a short period of time will cause the E-SHEIN E-gift cards locked for around 4-6 hrs. Once SHEIN E-gift cards locked, Kindly wait for 4-6 hours later to try.
    3. Limitation of site
    Gift cards purchased after March 3rd, 2021 can only be used on the original site it was purchased from. For example, if you purchased a gift card on SHEIN’s US site, it can only be redeemed on SHEIN US site(Including APP).

    Product & Stock

    "How do I choose my size?"
    You may enter in your measurements info via 'Check My Size', we will give you a recommendation based on the size that people like you bought. Additionally, you can find the most suitable size for yourself according to the 'Size Guide'.
    Note:
    1. Most items have this function, but not all.
    2. One Size doesn't mean fit all, it means only one size available.


    "How do I leave a review?"
    Here are the steps:
    1. Locate the order in the 'My orders'.
    2. Click the 'Review' button to leave a review of the item(s) you've purchased.
    For more info, please refer to 'Review Guidance'. Note: You can click the 'Confirm Delivery' button to collect SHEIN points after receiving your order.


    "Where do you ship from?"
    SHEIN ships worldwide. We have several warehouses located in Asia, North America, Europe and The Middle East.
    Typically, we will ship your packages from the nearest warehouse that has your items in stock.
    Please note: In the event your items are stocked in different warehouses, your order may be shipped in separate packages.
